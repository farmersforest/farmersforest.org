Farmers Forest

A forest of unique trees

Farmers Forest is a project that was founded to raise awareness of the low energy consumption of the Chia blockchain and help to offset that further. No two trees are the same in our forest. Each one will be a unique NFT and available on the Chia network.

Building our own forest

For every NFT tree minted, trees will be planted in the real world. Those trees will create our own forest to reduce the footprint of the Chia network through offsetting.

Available in 2022

Our NFT trees are not available, yet! We're eagerly awaiting the launch of the NFT protocol on the Chia network. When that's available, we'll be ready to release the NFT trees in our forest.

Built by farmers for the community

The founders of Farmers Forest have been Chia farmers since the beginning of the project and are still actively farming today.
